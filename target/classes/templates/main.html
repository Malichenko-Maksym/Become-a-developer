Character sequences that consist of latin or cyrillic letters, digits or symbols:" `'()+-_ "

are considered as a words. All other symbols(as well as whitespaces) are considered as

a separators that separate words from each other.

Program is case case-sensitive, symbols that have different Unicode, are considered as different

(even if they are looking the same).

Program works in the left-to-right manner.

If word doesn't have any unique symbols, it's skipped.

If there is no unique symbol in the final array of unique symbols, message like that will appear:

"First unique char from the array of unique chars is undefined"
